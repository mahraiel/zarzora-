About Our School

Our Specialties

We are a fully licensed daycare under the CDSS (California Department of Social Services) and have been in business for over 16 years. We are certified in early childhood education, as well as CPR and first aid.

We offer both pick up and drop off services from the children's school, Monday-Saturday, and (2) hot meals with two snacks a day.

We work with all age ranges from (Newborns-12 years old) in a 1:4 (adult:children) setting. We have learning activities for each stage of development. Outside play time 2x a day to give the children time to get fresh air. We accommodate families prone to allergies or who have disabilities.

Our History

Established in 2006, we started our business in Santa Monica 13 years ago. We have recently moved to a beautiful new location in Woodland Hills this September.
