Teen After School

12 - 16 YEARS

Our Teen After School Program is specially designed for middle and high school students (ages 12-16) who need a safe, engaging space after the school day ends. We combine academic support with enriching activities that help teens grow socially, emotionally, and intellectually.

With a perfect balance of structure and independence, we provide opportunities for leadership, skill-building, and fun in a supportive environment.

Academic Support

Homework help, tutoring, and study skills workshops with certified instructors

Tech & STEM

Coding, robotics, app development, and science exploration

Career Prep

Resume building, interview skills, and career exploration

Fitness

Sports, yoga, and wellness activities to stay active
