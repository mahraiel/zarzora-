After School Program

K - 12 YEARS

Our After School Program provides a safe, engaging environment for school-age children to learn, grow, and have fun when the school day ends. We offer a perfect blend of academic support, enrichment activities, and recreational time for students from Kindergarten through 12th grade.

With age-appropriate activities and dedicated staff, we help children develop new skills, complete homework, and build meaningful friendships in a nurturing environment.

Homework help & academic support
STEM activities & technology projects
Creative arts & self-expression
Sports & physical activities
Healthy snacks provided
Social skills development

Age-Specific Programming:

K-2nd Grade: Story time, basic STEM experiments, arts & crafts, outdoor play
3rd-5th Grade: Homework club, coding basics, team sports, creative writing
Middle School: Academic tutoring, robotics, leadership activities, art studio
High School: Study hall, college prep, career exploration, volunteer opportunities
